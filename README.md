# 🔥 Reliable Distributors Website

A responsive business website designed for **Reliable Distributors**, a company that provides **fire safety equipment and related services**.  
The website highlights the company's products, services, and customer feedback while offering an easy way for customers to get in touch.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🧯 **Product Showcase** – Displays available fire safety equipment
- 🛠 **Services Section** – Highlights services offered by the company
- ⭐ **Customer Testimonials** – Shows feedback from satisfied clients
- 💬 **WhatsApp Contact Button** – Quick and direct communication
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
